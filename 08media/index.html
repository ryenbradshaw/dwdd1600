About Me

Hello! My name is Ryen Bradshaw and I am currently a Junior at Utah Valley University. In my free time I love to coach volleyball, be outside, and binge reality T.V. shows. I also love taking up new hobbies to help me continue learning new art skills. My current obsession is print making and it's the funnest thing. I recommend looking into new hobbies to continue developing your artsy side!

As for my art career, I always loved art when growing up, but was unsure if I wanted to pursue it as a career. After taking some classes in college, I realized that I did want to do something artsy because I love being hands on. After a few semsesters of many art classes, I started seriously thinking which direction I would want to go. I was constantly pondering and researching and landed on Graphic Design and digital work. I loved creating logos and layouts for brands, as well as website design. I really enjoy the more graphic and simplicitic style and have loved perfecting my craft. I can't wait to continue learning about what I love, and start working in graphic arts!

Program Use

What is Adobe Illustrator best for?
Cartoon art, layouts such as diagrams, charts, and graphs, along with logos and illustration.
What is Adobe InDesign best for?
Layouts such as magazines, posters, or anything that needs to include spreads.
What is Adobe Photoshop best for?
Adjusting photos by cropping, correcting, or editing along with cutting around objects needed for other Adobe programs.
What is Procreate best for? SOURCE
Mapping out ideas by sketching, it's also really easy to create various layouts in a fast and easy way.
